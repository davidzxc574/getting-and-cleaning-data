#This code book includes and explains all variables in "test and train average.txt" under the same repository

#Explanation of variables
Variable "subject" includes different subjects in the HAR experiments. Each unique number represent a unqiue subjec. Its range is from 1 to 30.
Variable "activity" includes different activities recorded in the experiemnt. Values of this variable are descriptive with characters

Other variables are averages of mean and standard deviation of various measurements recorded during the experiments.  
The measurements have been normalized and bounded within [-1,1] before being averaged.
